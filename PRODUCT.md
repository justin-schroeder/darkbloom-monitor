# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Darkbloom providers running a Mac as compute capacity. They use the app from the menu bar while doing other work and need a fast answer to whether the provider is online, earning, healthy, and controllable without opening Terminal.

## Product Purpose

Darkbloom Monitor is a native macOS menu bar utility for observing and controlling the local Darkbloom provider. Success means the menu bar glyph and first panel view communicate the provider state at a glance, while deeper sections expose earnings, serving, health, activity, and fleet detail on demand.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, operational. The interface should feel like a polished Mac instrument: dense enough for repeated use, restrained enough to trust, and modern enough to match current Apple platform materials.

## Anti-references

Avoid a squeezed web dashboard, decorative glassmorphism, broad purple or blue gradient washes, custom fake controls, and telemetry cards that fight the platform. Do not hide critical warnings behind personalization.

## Design Principles

- Glance first, inspect second.
- Preserve native Mac controls and menu-bar behavior.
- Keep provider control paths explicit and hard to misuse.
- Separate live runtime truth from user presentation preferences.
- Surface degraded states even when the user prefers a compact menu.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Honor system light/dark appearance, Reduce Transparency, Increase Contrast, and Reduce Motion. Pair color-coded status with text and symbols, keep controls keyboard and VoiceOver accessible, and keep dense metadata readable without relying on very small text for essential information.
